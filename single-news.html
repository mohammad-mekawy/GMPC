Invitation to submit papers

GMPC is committed to publish high-quality journals, conference proceedings, and books in different aspects of life sciences to improve knowledge dissemination. We are pleased to inform you that German Multidisciplinary Publishing Center (GMPC), an academic publisher center founded in Germany, starts to accept paper submission in the new journals launched:

  • German Journal of Veterinary Research: covering the latest developments in animal research such as Microbiology, Infectious Diseases, Pharmacology, Biochemistry, Toxicology, Surgery, Clinical Research, Anatomy, Histology, Physiology, Animal Production, Nutrition and Genetics
  • German Journal of Microbiology: covering all aspects of research on bacterial, viral and fungal diseases of either humans or non-humans’ origin. Research focusing on diagnosis, detection, host response, pathogenesis, control, prevention, and treatment of microbial diseases in humans and animals are accepted.
  • GMPC Thesis & Opinion Platform: for master and PhD theses reviews as well as scientific opinions in English language with the aim of expanding the dissemination of research worldwide, especially theses that are difficult to access internationally.

GMPC follows fast track publication concept and all submitted manuscripts will be reviewed without selection bias. Submitted manuscripts will be thoroughly evaluated by the dedicated reviewers and editors that are selected among the best researchers in their areas of expertise. Manuscripts can be submitted online.
